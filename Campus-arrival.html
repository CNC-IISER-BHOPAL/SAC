Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admissions in IISER Bhopal.

What do I need to bring to campus? +

ID Proof (+ Photocopies, at least 2)for self and parents ● Insurance Policy Copy ● Bedsheets(can also be bought upon arrival) ● Blanket (can also be bought uponarrival) ● Clothes ● Toiletries (Soap, Shampoo,Toothbrush and paste, etc) ● Sanitizer ● Mask ● Medicines ● Pillow case ● Stationary

What can I buy on campus upon arrival? +

Bucket and mug ● Pillow ● Spoons ● Comb ● Lock and key ● Broom ● Soap case ● Dustbin ● Clothes hangers ● Clothes pins ● Bottles and various containers ● Cycles ● Quilt for winter ● Curtains ● Doormats ● Mattress ● Mirror ● Lab coat and goggles

With what amenities is the campus equipped, to help me settle in better? +

The campus is quite student friendly and will provide a smooth hostel transition. You will be guided to areas where you can buy mattresses and cycles along with general supplies like mugs, buckets, curtains, etc. We suggest you have a look at these services and if you still feel something missing, you can arrange for it from nearby markets in the city. We’d advise you to bring a laptop with you, but if you can’t arrange one, there is a computer center that offers desktops for use. Lifestyle services offered include a regular laundry and ironing service, E-Rickshaws and quite a few options to eat out.

What facilities will I be provided with, in my room? +

Every student is allotted a room to be shared with another student. A room will contain an almirah, 2 cots, 1 study table with 3 drawers, a LAN port, a curtain rod above the window, two slots to fit light bulbs/LED bulbs in addition to a tube light. Each student will be required to buy his or her own mattress, curtains, bucket, etc. There are 2 sets of bathrooms on each floor and 2 elevators working 24x7 in the hostel. Wi-Fi routers are present throughout each floor, to provide internet access. There is an air-conditioned common room for every hostel which has a television set and a refrigerator free to be used by any member of the hostel. There is also a Laundry room in each hostel equipped with washing machines and a dryer on a pay-per-use basis. For further details, you may contact the caretaker or warden of your hostel.

Should I be worried about ragging on campus? What are the institute policies regarding this? +

Ragging is practically non-existent on campus, and there is absolutely nothing to worry about. Before entering college, there is a compulsory Anti-Ragging form that needs to be filled and signed by each student. There is an active and responsive Anti-Ragging Cell on campus where complaints can be registered and quick, effective and immediate action will be taken to sort out the issue. There is also a Counselling Cell where students can go if and when they feel that they need a safe, comforting and experienced voice. This includes Peer Counsellors of various batches.

How are the sports facilities on campus? +

The campus provides a large array of sports facilities for everyone to use. We have two basketball courts, a football field, two tennis courts, a cricket ground, a Kho-Kho and Kabaddi playing field and a badminton court. We have yoga, athletics, and a well-equipped gym. We also have trained coaches for sports, to help interested students hone themselves. The institute participates in the annual Inter-IISER Sports Meet (IISM) which is a multi-sport event in which all the IISERs, IISc, NISER and CEBS compete. Sangharsh (the intra-IISER meet), the Inter-Hostel Tournament and various weekend Mini Tournaments have garnered immense support and participation from the students as well as the faculties
