About the Tournament

The Sangharsh'25 (IntraSports Tournament) is an annual event bringing together sports enthusiasts across various disciplines for friendly competition, skill challenges, and team building. Experience thrilling matches, outstanding performances, and community spirit.

  • Multiple sports categories
  • Professional refereeing
  • Exciting prizes
  • Networking opportunities

This year, we are proud to feature 17 diverse sports that cater to both team players and individual competitors. Our lineup is designed to challenge and inspire, ensuring there’s something for everyone. Our commitment extends beyond the tournament. We strive to promote healthy living, nurture local talent, and strengthen community bonds. Whether you’re competing on the field or cheering from the stands, Sangharsh'25 is an opportunity to be part of a legacy that celebrates sportsmanship and unity. Whether you are competing or cheering from the sidelines, Sangharsh'25 promises an experience filled with passion, excitement, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. Join us as we celebrate sportsmanship and unity through every match, game, and performance.
